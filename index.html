We're a takeaway with a big heart and a blazing wood fire at our core. Based in the heart of Worcester at 62 Stockenstrom Street, BBQ Express is where the Breede Valley comes to eat properly.

From crispy wood-fired pizzas and stacked Gatsby's to fall-off-the-bone chicken wings and thick-cut steaks... every dish is made fresh, with fire, and with flavour in mind. And yes, everything on our menu is 100% Halaal.
